    Jiameng Lyu

I am a third-year PhD student majoring in Applied Mathematics at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center and Department of Mathematical Sciences at Tsinghua University, and I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yuan Zhou. Prior to Tsinghua, I received my B.S. in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China with the highest honor (Guo Moruo Scholarship, top 1%) in July 2021.

My research interests center around data-driven decision-making including online learning, online optimization, and statistical machine learning, with a particular emphasis on the applications to inventory and revenue management.

Here is my CV. Feel free to contact me through Email (lvjm21@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn).
