    Jiameng Lyu

I am a third year PhD student majoring in Applied Mathematics at Yau Mathematical Sciences Center and Department of Mathematical Sciences at Tsinghua University, and I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Yuan Zhou. Prior to Tsinghua, I received my B.S. in Statistics from University of Science and Technology of China with the highest honor (Guo Muoruo Scholarship) in July 2021.

My research interests lie broadly in online learning, online optimization, statistical machine learning and causal inference, with a particular emphasis on the application of these methods in the field of inventory and revenue management.

Email: lvjm21@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn. If you want to have a chat, always feel free to contact me through Email.
